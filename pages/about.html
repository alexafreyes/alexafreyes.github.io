About the Artist

I am a multimedia artist born and raised in New Jersey and third year undergraduate student at the Mason Gross School of the Arts concentrating in design. I intends to graduate May of 2021 with a minor in Women's and Gender Studies (permitting COVID-19 allows me to).

My work explores interpersonal connections, the dichotomy between empathy and capitalism, and the self-image. I'm inspired by my personal relationships with institutions and my ability to analyze them more deeply throughout my time studying at Rutgers.
