About the Artist

Alexa Reyes was born and raised in New Jersey. She is a multimedia artist and third year undergraduate student at the Mason Gross School of the Arts concentrating in design. She intends to grduate May of 2021 with a minor in Women's and Gender Studies.

Her work explores interpersonal connections, the dichotomy between empathy and capitalism, and the self-image.
