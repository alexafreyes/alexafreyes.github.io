Object Book

(2018)

5inx7.5in

Final project booklet based on initial object study. Object chosen was an old pair of glasses.

I wanted to show how glasses affect a person's face, especially if the prescription is as high as mine. Additionally, I was exploring how light travels and transforms in the clear frames and lenses.
