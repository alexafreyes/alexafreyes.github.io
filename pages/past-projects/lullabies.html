Lullabies

Print I-A (2019)

Silkscreen on 2inx2in acrlyics discs hung on a metal mobile

Printed on the clear discs are lyrics from popular songs that have existential undertones.
My idea for this project was to show how music is such a powerful art form and tool, especially in developing years but their messages are often ignored or underanalyzed.
