Chicago Humanities Festival Lecture Series

(2021)

Lecture series branding identity concept and design

The branding concept for the 2018 Chicago Humanities Festival draws from the theme of “Graphic!”, where its definition goes beyond the typical visual association. Moments of disjointed and glitchy type call back to lecture topics such as the dark web and black market. The pairing of these elements along with bright, enticing colors reflects the bleak reality of what “graphic” can truly mean.
